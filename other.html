Identity Project

The display I decided to make is a tree. I represent the tree. The roots are where I came from, my origins and background. My origin is from the Czech Republic, which I explained in my Czech writing. The trunk of the tree symbolizes my body and my physical character and the branches are my experiences and the mental side of me. Each branch represents my different strengths and passions. That's why I hung the symbols of my interests on them.
